## What the Infographic Shows

The infographic is titled **"Be Digitally Literate: A Student's Guide"** and is designed as a single-page visual resource for first-year B.Tech students. It covers three main topics:

1. **What is Digital Literacy?**
   - Ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies
   - Skills needed: information literacy, media literacy, online safety, and communication

2. **Safe Internet Practices**
   - Use strong, unique passwords for every account
   - Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
   - Never share OTPs or personal data on unverified sites
   - Recognise phishing emails and suspicious links

3. **Professional Online Presence**
   - Set up profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle
   - Keep all public profiles professional and up to date
   - Avoid sharing sensitive personal information publicly
   - Think before you post — your digital footprint is permanent
